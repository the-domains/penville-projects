Penville Wine

75% of fermentation is whole cluster in a 476 gallon cement egg then transferred to neutral puncheon for six months. The remainder is open top fermented with 30% whole cluster then transferred to 1200L barrel for six months. After blending the wine was refined further in puncheon for ten months prior to bottling.
